Map: Trolley and Bus Routes in Hartford, CT, 1910-1943

Explore this interactive map to see how trolleys (red lines) were replaced by buses (blue lines) in the Hartford region. Click the tabs or use arrow keys to advance years. Hover over lines for route details. View data files compiled by Adam Polinski for his master's thesis at the University of Connecticut in 2015. Learn more at OnTheLine.trincoll.edu.
